SENSOR DRIVER NOTICE
====================

During installation, a kernel sensor driver (PawnIO) will be installed
to enable hardware monitoring — CPU temperature, power, and clock speeds.

  - PawnIO is part of the LibreHardwareMonitor project
  - It is digitally signed by Microsoft (WHQL)
  - It does not connect to the internet or collect any data
  - It is automatically removed when you uninstall Pulse
